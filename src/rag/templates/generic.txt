GENERIC DOCUMENT EXTRACTION SCHEMA
====================================

Document Type: generic
Purpose: Extract structured information from any document that does not fit
the medical or financial categories — e.g. legal agreements, policy documents,
research reports, memos, correspondence, product manuals, RFPs.

SCHEMA FIELDS
-------------
- title (string | null)
  The primary heading or title of the document.
  Look for the largest/first heading, a "Subject:" line, or a cover-page title.

- summary (string)
  A 2–4 sentence plain-English summary of the document's purpose, scope, and
  key conclusions or actions. Do not reproduce raw text verbatim.

- key_entities (list of NamedEntity objects)
  Important named entities found in the document. Each entity has:
    - text (string): entity text as it appears in the document.
    - entity_type (string | null): one of PERSON, ORG, LOCATION, DATE, PRODUCT,
      LAW, EVENT, or MISC.

- dates_mentioned (list of strings)
  All significant dates in the document, in the format found.
  Include effective dates, expiry dates, submission deadlines, signatures dates.

- extracted_fields (dict[string, any])
  A flexible key-value map for domain-specific fields that stand out, such as:
    - contract_number, reference_id, case_number
    - total_value, penalty_clause, governing_law
    - document_version, confidentiality_level
  Keys must be lowercase_snake_case and descriptive.

- extraction_confidence (float 0.0–1.0)
  - 0.9–1.0: title, summary, and at least 3 entities extracted.
  - 0.5–0.8: title and summary extracted but entity list is sparse.
  - < 0.5: document is mostly unreadable, scanned, or entirely images.

- extraction_notes (string)
  Any caveats or unusual characteristics of the document.

-------------------------------
Input text excerpt:
  "SERVICE AGREEMENT
   Reference: SA-2025-1138

   This Service Agreement ('Agreement') is entered into as of January 1, 2025,
   by and between Pinnacle Solutions Inc. ('Service Provider'), a corporation
   incorporated under the laws of Ontario, Canada, and Redwood Analytics Corp.
   ('Client'), incorporated under the laws of Delaware, USA.

   Term: January 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025.
   Total Contract Value: USD 120,000
   Governing Law: Province of Ontario, Canada."

Expected output:
{
  "document_type": "generic",
  "title": "SERVICE AGREEMENT",
  "summary": "A one-year service agreement between Pinnacle Solutions Inc. (Service Provider) and Redwood Analytics Corp. (Client) for USD 120,000. The agreement runs from January 1 to December 31, 2025, and is governed by the laws of Ontario, Canada.",
  "key_entities": [
    {"text": "Pinnacle Solutions Inc.", "entity_type": "ORG"},
    {"text": "Redwood Analytics Corp.", "entity_type": "ORG"},
    {"text": "Ontario, Canada",         "entity_type": "LOCATION"},
    {"text": "Delaware, USA",           "entity_type": "LOCATION"}
  ],
  "dates_mentioned": ["January 1, 2025", "December 31, 2025"],
  "extracted_fields": {
    "reference_id": "SA-2025-1138",
    "total_contract_value": "USD 120,000",
    "governing_law": "Province of Ontario, Canada",
    "contract_term": "January 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025"
  },
  "extraction_confidence": 0.92,
  "extraction_notes": ""
}

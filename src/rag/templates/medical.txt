MEDICAL DOCUMENT EXTRACTION SCHEMA
====================================

Document Type: medical
Purpose: Extract structured patient and clinical information from medical forms,
clinical notes, discharge summaries, lab reports, and prescriptions.

SCHEMA FIELDS
-------------
- patient_name (string | null)
  The full name of the patient.
  Look for labels: "Patient:", "Name:", "Patient Name:", "Pt:", "Patient ID".

- date_of_birth (string | null)
  Patient's date of birth. Preserve the exact format found in the document.
  Look for: "DOB:", "Date of Birth:", "Birth Date:", "D.O.B.".

- diagnosis_codes (list of strings)
  ICD-10 or ICD-9 alphanumeric codes. Example: ["E11.9", "I10", "Z87.891"].
  Look for: "Diagnosis Code:", "ICD:", "DX Code:", "Principal Diagnosis:".

- diagnosis_descriptions (list of strings)
  Plain-text descriptions that correspond to the codes above.
  Example: ["Type 2 diabetes mellitus without complications", "Essential hypertension"].

- medications (list of Medication objects)
  Each medication must have:
    - name (string): Drug name, generic or brand.
    - dosage (string | null): Dose amount and unit, e.g. "500 mg", "10 mcg".
    - frequency (string | null): e.g. "twice daily", "QID", "every 8 hours", "PRN".

- provider_name (string | null)
  The treating physician or clinician.
  Look for: "Physician:", "Provider:", "Ordering Provider:", "Attending:", "Signed by:".

- visit_date (string | null)
  Date of visit, encounter, or service. Preserve original format.
  Look for: "Date of Service:", "Visit Date:", "Encounter Date:", "Admission Date:".

- facility (string | null)
  Name of the hospital, clinic, or medical centre.
  Often appears in the document header/letterhead or footer.

- extraction_confidence (float 0.0–1.0)
  How complete and reliable this extraction is:
  - 0.9–1.0: all key fields (patient_name, visit_date, at least one diagnosis) found.
  - 0.5–0.8: some key fields missing but core structure is present.
  - < 0.5: major fields missing or document is ambiguous.

- extraction_notes (string)
  Note any ambiguities, e.g. "No diagnosis codes found; used description only."


FEW-SHOT EXAMPLE 1
-------------------
Input text excerpt:
  "St. Mary's Medical Center
   Patient: Jane Doe          DOB: 07/22/1978
   Visit Date: 2024-03-05
   Attending Physician: Dr. Robert Chen, MD

   Diagnoses:
     E11.9  Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus without complications
     I10    Essential Hypertension

   Medications:
   - Metformin HCl  1000 mg  twice daily
   - Amlodipine     5 mg     once daily"

Expected output:
{
  "document_type": "medical",
  "patient_name": "Jane Doe",
  "date_of_birth": "07/22/1978",
  "diagnosis_codes": ["E11.9", "I10"],
  "diagnosis_descriptions": [
    "Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus without complications",
    "Essential Hypertension"
  ],
  "medications": [
    {"name": "Metformin HCl", "dosage": "1000 mg", "frequency": "twice daily"},
    {"name": "Amlodipine",    "dosage": "5 mg",    "frequency": "once daily"}
  ],
  "provider_name": "Dr. Robert Chen, MD",
  "visit_date": "2024-03-05",
  "facility": "St. Mary's Medical Center",
  "extraction_confidence": 0.97,
  "extraction_notes": ""
}
